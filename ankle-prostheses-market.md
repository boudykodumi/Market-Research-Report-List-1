<p><strong>The Ankle Prostheses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cementless</li><li>Cemented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ankle prostheses market is primarily divided into cementless and cemented types. Cementless prostheses utilize a porous surface that promotes bone ingrowth, offering long-term stability without the use of adhesive materials. Conversely, cemented prostheses involve using bone cement to secure the implant, ensuring immediate stability and quick rehabilitation. Each type has distinct advantages, with cementless designs favoring biological integration over time, while cemented options provide rapid fixation, addressing different patient needs and surgical preferences.</p></p>
