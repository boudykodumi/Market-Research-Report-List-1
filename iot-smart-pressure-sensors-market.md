<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Diffused Silicon Type</li><li>Sapphire Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT smart pressure sensors market comprises several types, including piezoelectric, diffused silicon, and sapphire sensors. Piezoelectric sensors detect pressure changes through electrical charge generation, offering high sensitivity and rapid response. Diffused silicon sensors utilize silicon diaphragm technology for accurate pressure measurement and temperature stability. Sapphire sensors leverage the hardness and thermal stability of sapphire for extreme conditions, providing durability and precision. Together, these technologies cater to diverse industrial applications, driving innovation in the IoT landscape.</p></p>
