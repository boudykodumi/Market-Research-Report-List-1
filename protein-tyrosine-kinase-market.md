<p><p>The Protein Tyrosine Kinase (PTK) market is divided into two main types: receptor-type and non-receptor type. Receptor-type PTKs are membrane-bound enzymes activated by ligand binding, playing critical roles in cellular signaling and growth regulation. Non-receptor PTKs, located in the cytoplasm, are involved in various intracellular signaling pathways, influencing numerous cellular processes. Both types are crucial in therapeutic applications, particularly in cancer treatment, where their dysregulation often contributes to tumor progression and metastasis.</p></p>
