<p><p>The Magnesium Testing Reagent Market is segmented into Serum Testing and Urine Testing. Urine Testing measures the magnesium excretion rate, providing insights into renal function and the body's magnesium status. Both testing types are essential for clinical diagnostics, with applications in monitoring patients with chronic diseases, dietary deficiencies, and assessing magnesium supplementation efficacy.</p></p>
