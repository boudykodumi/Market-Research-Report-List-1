<p><p>The biodegradable copolyesters market primarily includes polybutylene succinate (PBS) and polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA). PBS is derived from renewable resources and provides excellent thermal properties, making it suitable for packaging and agricultural films. PHA, produced by microbial fermentation, boasts superior biodegradation capabilities and is often used in medical applications and food packaging. Both materials are gaining traction due to increasing environmental concerns and sustainability initiatives, positioning them as alternatives to conventional plastics across various industries.</p></p>
