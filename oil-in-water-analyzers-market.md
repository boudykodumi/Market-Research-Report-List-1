<p><p>The Oil-in-Water Analyzers market comprises various technologies to detect and quantify oil concentrations in water. Ultrasound analyzers use sound waves to identify oil droplets based on their size and distribution. UV fluorescence analyzers utilize ultraviolet light to excite oil molecules, allowing for quantitative measurement based on emitted fluorescence. Other methods may include infrared spectroscopy and laser-induced breakdown spectroscopy, each varying in sensitivity, specificity, and application, catering to diverse industrial and environmental monitoring needs.</p></p>
