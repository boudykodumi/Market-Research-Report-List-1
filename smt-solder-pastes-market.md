<p><p>The SMT solder pastes market is primarily divided into two types: lead-bearing and lead-free solder pastes. Lead-bearing SMT solder pastes contain lead as a primary alloying element, offering excellent wetting properties and reliability, typically used in traditional electronics manufacturing. Conversely, lead-free SMT solder pastes replace lead with alternative metals like tin, silver, and copper, promoting environmental safety and compliance with regulations. The growing demand for eco-friendly products is driving the adoption of lead-free alternatives in various industries.</p></p>
