<p><strong>The Industrial Radiography Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Radiography</li><li>Computed Radiography</li><li>Direct Radiography</li><li>Computed Tomography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial radiography testing market includes various technologies for non-destructive testing. Film Radiography uses traditional film to capture images, providing high-resolution results but requiring chemical processing. Computed Radiography employs reusable imaging plates, allowing digital conversion of film images for enhanced analysis. Direct Radiography captures images directly onto digital sensors, enabling immediate results and advanced image processing. Computed Tomography offers 3D imaging by taking multiple X-ray projections, providing detailed internal structure analysis, making it valuable for complex components.</p></p>
