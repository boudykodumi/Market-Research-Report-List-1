<p><p>The jet pumps market features primarily two types: vertical and horizontal jet pumps. Vertical jet pumps utilize a vertical orientation, making them efficient for deep well applications, often favored in agricultural and municipal water supply systems. On the other hand, horizontal jet pumps are designed for surface-level installations, ideal for applications requiring moderate lift and flow rates. Both types leverage jet propulsion to enhance fluid movement, catering to diverse industrial, commercial, and residential needs. Overall, their design influences performance and application suitability.</p></p>
