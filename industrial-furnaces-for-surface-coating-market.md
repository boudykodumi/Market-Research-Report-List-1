<p><p>Industrial furnaces for surface coating are categorized mainly into combustion and electric types. Combustion-type furnaces use fossil fuels like natural gas or oil to generate heat, offering high efficiency and rapid heating. These are widely used for large-scale operations and applications requiring intense heat. Electric-type furnaces, on the other hand, utilize electric resistance or induction heating, providing precise temperature control and cleaner operation. They are ideal for smaller batches and sensitive materials, making them suitable for specialized surface coating processes.</p></p>
