<p><p>The Neodymium-Iron-Boron (NdFeB) magnet market is primarily segmented into two types: sintered and bonded NdFeB magnets. Sintered NdFeB magnets are produced through a sintering process, resulting in high magnetic strength and density, making them ideal for applications requiring robust performance. Conversely, bonded NdFeB magnets are created by combining NdFeB powder with a polymer binder, offering flexibility in shape and size, along with enhanced corrosion resistance. Each type caters to different industry needs, such as automotive, consumer electronics, and renewable energy.</p></p>
