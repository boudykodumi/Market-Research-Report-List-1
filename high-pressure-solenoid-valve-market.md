<p><ul><li>Straight Moving Type</li><li>Pilot Operated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-pressure solenoid valve market primarily consists of two types: straight moving type and pilot-operated type. Straight moving valves directly control fluid flow through the solenoid mechanism, providing quick actuation and reliable performance in high-pressure conditions. Pilot-operated valves, on the other hand, use a small pilot valve to control a larger flow, offering greater versatility and efficiency, especially in larger systems. Both types serve critical roles in various industrial applications, including hydraulics, pneumatics, and automation.</p></p>
