<p><p>The IP-based PBX market is primarily categorized into Unlimited PBX and Metered PBX systems. Unlimited PBX offers a flat-rate pricing model, allowing businesses to enjoy unrestricted call features and services for a monthly fee, ideal for high call volume users. Conversely, Metered PBX charges based on actual usage, making it suitable for organizations with sporadic calling needs, as costs are tied to the number of calls made. Both models provide flexibility and scalability for diverse business requirements.</p></p>
