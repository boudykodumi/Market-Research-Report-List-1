<p><ul><li>UV Tape</li><li>Non-UV Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Packaging Cut Tape market includes two primary types: UV Tape and Non-UV Tape. UV Tape is designed for applications that require exposure to ultraviolet light for curing adhesives or for protection during semiconductor fabrication. It offers superior adhesion and thermal stability. Non-UV Tape, on the other hand, is utilized in applications where UV exposure is not required, providing a reliable hold without the curing process. Both types are crucial for ensuring the integrity and efficiency of semiconductor packaging processes.</p></p>
