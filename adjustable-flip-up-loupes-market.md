<p><p>The Adjustable Flip-Up Loupes market primarily includes two types: Galilean and Prismatic lenses. Galilean loupes offer a lightweight design with a simple optical system, providing magnification suitable for various tasks. In contrast, Prismatic loupes utilize prisms to achieve higher magnification with a more compact design, enhancing depth perception. Both types allow for adjustability, enabling users to customize the fit and angle for optimal comfort and vision. These loupes are essential tools in dentistry, surgery, and other precision-related fields.</p></p>
