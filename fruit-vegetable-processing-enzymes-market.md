<p><p>The fruit and vegetable processing enzymes market encompasses various types, including amylase, pectinase, protease, and cellulase. Amylase breaks down starch into sugars, enhancing sweetness in juices. Pectinase improves fruit juice extraction and clarity by breaking down pectin in cell walls. Protease aids in tenderizing fruits and vegetables, enhancing texture. Cellulase helps in reducing viscosity and improving quality in processing, by hydrolyzing cellulose. Together, these enzymes enhance flavor, texture, and quality, driving efficiency in the food industry.</p></p>
