<p><strong>The Solar PV Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocrystalline</li><li>Polycrystalline</li><li>And Thin-Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar PV panel market comprises three primary types: monocrystalline, polycrystalline, and thin-film. Monocrystalline panels, made from single-crystal silicon, offer high efficiency and space-saving design, making them ideal for limited areas. Polycrystalline panels, composed of multiple silicon crystals, are generally less efficient but more cost-effective, suitable for larger installations. Thin-film panels utilize a variety of materials, offering lightweight and flexible options but typically lower efficiency, making them suitable for specific applications like building-integrated photovoltaics and large-scale setups.</p></p>
