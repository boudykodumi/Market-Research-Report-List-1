<p><p>The textile machine lubricants market is categorized into two main types: mineral oil-based and synthetic oil-based lubricants. Mineral oil-based lubricants are derived from refined petroleum products, offering good lubrication and protective properties at a lower cost. In contrast, synthetic oil-based lubricants are engineered from chemical compounds, providing superior performance, higher thermal stability, and longer service life. While mineral oils are widely used, synthetic oils are gaining traction due to their enhanced functionality and environmental considerations in textile machinery operations.</p></p>
