<p><strong>The Medical C-Arm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Mobile C-arm</li><li>FPD Mobile C-arm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical C-arm system market comprises two primary types: CCD Mobile C-arms and FPD Mobile C-arms. CCD Mobile C-arms utilize charge-coupled device technology for imaging, providing good-quality images at lower costs, making them suitable for various surgical applications. In contrast, FPD Mobile C-arms employ flat-panel detectors, offering superior image quality, reduced radiation exposure, and advanced features such as real-time imaging. Both types are essential in various medical settings, enhancing surgical accuracy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
