<p><ul><li>Negative Etch Resists</li><li>Thick Negative Resists</li><li>Negative Lift-Off Resists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The negative photoresist market encompasses several types tailored for specific applications. Negative etch resists are used in semiconductor fabrication, creating high-resolution patterns by removing exposed areas during etching. Thick negative resists are ideal for applications requiring substantial layer thickness, such as in microelectromechanical systems (MEMS). Negative lift-off resists facilitate the removal of unwanted metal layers after deposition, allowing for precise patterning in processes like circuit fabrication. These various types serve critical roles in modern photolithography techniques.</p></p>
