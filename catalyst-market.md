<p><p>Catalyst market types encompass various materials used to accelerate chemical reactions. Zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicates, widely utilized in petrochemical refining and adsorption applications. Metals, such as platinum and palladium, serve as effective catalysts in hydrogenation and oxidation processes. Chemical compounds, including acids and bases, provide catalytic activity in diverse reactions. Enzymes, as biocatalysts, facilitate biochemical processes in industrial and pharmaceutical applications. Organometallic materials combine organic and metal components to enhance catalytic efficiency in organic synthesis, making them crucial in modern chemistry.</p></p>
