<p><strong>The Hyperthermia Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Heat Delivery System</li><li>Internal Heat Delivery System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hyperthermia systems market is primarily segmented into two types: external heat delivery systems and internal heat delivery systems. External heat delivery systems utilize methods like radiofrequency, microwave, or infrared to apply heat to the body's surface, targeting tumors from outside. In contrast, internal heat delivery systems involve techniques such as intratumoral or intravenous heating, directly raising the temperature of the tumor or surrounding tissues. Both methods aim to enhance the efficacy of cancer treatments by improving tumor sensitivity to therapies.</p></p>
