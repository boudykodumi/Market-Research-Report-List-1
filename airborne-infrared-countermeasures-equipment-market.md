<p><p>The Airborne Infrared Countermeasures Equipment market is primarily divided into two main types: Interference Equipment and Early Warning Equipment. Interference Equipment is designed to disrupt or jam infrared signals from incoming threats, effectively misguiding them away from their intended targets. In contrast, Early Warning Equipment focuses on detection, providing advanced alerts about potential infrared threats, allowing aircraft to initiate defensive measures proactively. Together, these technologies enhance the survivability of airborne platforms against infrared-guided missiles and other similar threats.</p></p>
