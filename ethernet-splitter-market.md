<p><strong>The Ethernet Splitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ethernet splitter market comprises two main types: passive and active splitters. Passive splitters simply divide a single Ethernet cable into multiple outputs without any power source or signal amplification, making them cost-effective but limited in distance and performance. Active splitters, on the other hand, require power and use electronic circuitry to boost the signal, allowing for longer distances and better signal quality. Each type serves different networking needs, catering to various user requirements and environments.</p></p>
