<p><p>The automotive EVAP (Evaporative Emission Control) systems market includes several key components: </p><p>1. **Fuel Tank**: Stores fuel while minimizing vapor escape.</p><p>2. **Gas Cap**: Seals the fuel tank to prevent vapor leakage.</p><p>3. **Liquid Vapor Separator**: Distinguishes between liquid fuel and vapor, ensuring efficient emission control.</p><p>4. **Control Valve**: Regulates vapor flow between the tank and canister.</p><p>5. **Sensor**: Monitors vapor levels and system performance.</p><p>6. **Hoses**: Transport vapors safely within the system. </p><p>These components work together to reduce harmful emissions and enhance fuel efficiency.</p></p>
