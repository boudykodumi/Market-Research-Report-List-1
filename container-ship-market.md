<p><p>The container ship market consists of several types, including part-of-the-container ships, full container ships, and transformable container ships. Part-of-the-container ships are designed to carry partial loads, providing flexibility for shipping smaller quantities. Full container ships, or "feeder ships," transport high volumes of containers, optimizing space and route efficiency. Transformable container ships can adapt their design to accommodate various cargo needs, enhancing versatility in shipping operations. Together, these types cater to diverse logistical requirements in global trade.</p></p>
