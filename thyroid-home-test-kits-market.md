<p><p>The thyroid home test kits market includes various types, primarily TSH tests, FT4 tests, and FT3 tests. TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone) tests measure hormone levels that regulate thyroid function, indicating hypo- or hyperthyroidism. FT4 (Free Thyroxine) tests gauge the unbound form of thyroxine, crucial for metabolism and growth, while FT3 (Free Triiodothyronine) tests examine levels of the active thyroid hormone. These kits empower individuals to monitor their thyroid health conveniently at home, facilitating early diagnosis and management.</p></p>
