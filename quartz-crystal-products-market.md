<p><p>The Quartz Crystal Products market is primarily divided into two types: DIP (Dual In-line Package) and SMD (Surface Mount Device) types. DIP crystals have leads extending from both sides, making them suitable for through-hole mounting on circuit boards, typically used in traditional electronics. In contrast, SMD crystals are designed for surface mounting, providing a compact form factor that supports modern, space-efficient designs. Both types serve essential functions in frequency control and timing applications across various electronic devices.</p></p>
