<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission Equipment (FACTS) refers to advanced technologies that enhance the controllability and stability of power transmission systems. The market encompasses two primary types: shunt compensation and series compensation. Shunt compensation involves connecting devices like STATIC VAR Compensators (SVCs) to the grid, improving voltage stability and reactive power flow. In contrast, series compensation employs devices like series capacitors to enhance power transfer capability and regulate voltage across transmission lines, ultimately optimizing system performance and reliability.</p></p>
