<p><p>Porcelain stoneware is a type of ceramic material that is known for its durability, low porosity, and aesthetic versatility. It is primarily composed of finely-ground natural raw materials, such as kaolin, feldspar, and quartz, which are fired at high temperatures to achieve a dense, non-absorbent product. Due to its robust characteristics, porcelain stoneware is widely used for flooring, wall cladding, and outdoor surfaces.</p><p>The Porcelain Stoneware Market is experiencing significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.
