<p><p>Semiconductor wafer cutting machines are vital for processing silicon wafers into smaller chips used in electronics. Mechanical cutting employs diamond blades for precise slicing, ideal for larger volumes and cost-effectiveness but may introduce mechanical stress. In contrast, laser cutting utilizes high-powered lasers for precision cutting without physical contact, minimizing defects and allowing for intricate designs. While mechanical cutting is widely used for its efficiency, laser cutting is gaining traction due to its accuracy and adaptability in complex semiconductor applications.</p></p>
