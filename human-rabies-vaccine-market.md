<p><strong>The Human Rabies Vaccine Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Exposure Vaccine</li><li>Post-Exposure Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The human rabies vaccine market encompasses two primary applications: pre-exposure and post-exposure vaccines. Pre-exposure vaccines are administered to individuals at higher risk of rabies exposure, such as healthcare workers and travelers to endemic areas, to build immunity before potential exposure. In contrast, post-exposure vaccines are given after a confirmed or suspected rabies exposure, typically in conjunction with rabies immunoglobulin, to prevent disease onset. Both applications are crucial for preventing rabies, a fatal viral infection, emphasizing public health significance.</p></p>
