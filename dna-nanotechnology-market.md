<p><p>The DNA nanotechnology market comprises two primary types: structural DNA nanotechnology and dynamic DNA nanotechnology. Structural DNA nanotechnology focuses on the design and assembly of static nano-structures using DNA, which can serve as scaffolds or frameworks for various applications, including drug delivery and biosensing. In contrast, dynamic DNA nanotechnology involves programmable and responsive systems where DNA molecules can undergo conformational changes or perform specific functions, enabling advanced applications in molecular robotics, targeted therapy, and nanomachinery.</p></p>
