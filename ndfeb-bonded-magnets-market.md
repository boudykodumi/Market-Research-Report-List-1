<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Pressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NdFeB bonded magnets market consists of two primary manufacturing types: injection molding and pressing. Injection molding involves melting polymer and mixing it with NdFeB particles, which allows for complex shapes and high production rates. Pressing, on the other hand, utilizes a compacting process where NdFeB powder is pressed into shape under high pressure, often followed by sintering. Both methods enable cost-effective production of lightweight, powerful magnets suited for applications in electronics, automotive, and renewable energy sectors.</p></p>
